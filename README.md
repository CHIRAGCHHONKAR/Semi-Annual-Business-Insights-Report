## Semi-Annual-Business-Insights-Report

## Project Overview
An executive-level Power BI dashboard analyzing six months of business data to evaluate regional growth, profitability, and logistics performance.

## Key Business Insights
* **Profitability:** Successfully achieved and tracked a total profit of **8,041K**.
* **Revenue & Volume:** Analyzed **47.3M** in total sales across **10,000 orders**.
* **Regional Dominance:** Identified **Uttar Pradesh** and **Maharashtra** as the top-performing states by order count.
* **Logistics Efficiency:** Maintained a high delivery success rate of **85.54%**.
* **Payment Trends:** Analyzed a near-equal distribution between **UPI (32.45%)**, **Credit Card (33.65%)**, and **Cash on Delivery (33.9%)**.
* **Product Performance:** Top sales contributors include **Bluetooth Speakers**, **Curtains**, and **Power Banks**.

## Technical Skills Used
* **Power BI:** Data Modeling, DAX (Data Analysis Expressions), and Visualization.
* **Data Cleaning:** Power Query was used to handle missing values and format dates.
* **Business Intelligence:** Translated raw sales figures into actionable regional insights.
